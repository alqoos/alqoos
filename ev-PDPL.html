This page helps evaluate company data protection under PDPL.

Please respond effectively to all questions

1.Does the company clearly define the types of personal data it collects?

2.Are individuals informed when their personal data is collected?

3.Is explicit consent obtained from individuals before collecting their data?

4.Is personal data used only for the specific purposes for which it was collected?

5.Are individuals informed about the purposes for which their data will be used?

6.Does the company have policies to prevent unauthorized use of personal data?

7.Does the company use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data??

8.Is there a mechanism for reporting data breaches or security incidents involving personal data?

9.Are employees trained on data protection policies?

10.Does the company specify a clear retention period for personal data?

11.Are there procedures in place to delete data once it is no longer needed?

12.Is personal data stored only within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia?

13.Can individuals access their personal data stored by the company?

14.Does the company allow individuals to modify or delete their data upon request?

15.Does the company respond to individuals’ requests to exercise their rights within a reasonable timeframe?

16.Does the company share personal data with third parties?

17.If data is shared, does the company obtain prior consent from individuals?

18.Does the company implement safeguards when sharing data with external parties?

19.Has the company updated its policies to align with the requirements of the PDPL?

20.Are periodic reviews conducted on data protection policies to ensure compliance?

21.Has the company appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) to oversee compliance?

22.Is the company’s data protection policy publicly available?

23.Is the use of personal data explained in clear and simple language?

24.Is there a clear mechanism for receiving individuals’ complaints regarding data protection?

25.Are complaints handled fairly and in a timely manner?
